Menu

Welcome to Food Shop Restaurant!

Explore Our Menu

Appetizers
    Classic Bruschetta: Toasted baguette slices topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.
    Crispy Calamari: Lightly breaded and fried calamari rings served with homemade marinara sauce.
    wow momos: Tender chinese food momos in mozzarella and sauce.
Salads
    Caesar Salad: Crisp romaine lettuce, homemade croutons, parmesan cheese, and tangy Caesar dressing.
    Greek Salad: Fresh mixed greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and a zesty Greek dressing.
    Caprese Salad: Slices of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves drizzled with balsamic glaze.
Pizzas
    Margherita Pizza: Classic Neapolitan-style pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, and a hint of olive oil.
    Pepperoni Pizza: Traditional pizza topped with generous amounts of pepperoni and melted mozzarella cheese.
    BBQ mozzarella Pizza: Tangy barbecue sauce, grilled cheese, red onions, and cilantro on a thin crust.
Pasta
    Spaghetti Bolognese: Al dente spaghetti pasta served with a rich and hearty meat sauce.
    Fettuccine Alfredo: Creamy Parmesan sauce tossed with fettuccine pasta and topped with fresh parsley.
    Penne Arrabiata: Penne pasta tossed in a spicy tomato sauce with garlic, chili flakes, and fresh basil.
Main Courses
    Grilled Salmon: Fresh salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce and roasted vegetables.
    New York Strip Steak: Juicy grilled New York strip steak served with garlic mashed potatoes and sautéed spinach.
    vegan momos: fried with marinara sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and served with spaghetti.
Desserts
    Tiramisu: Traditional Italian dessert made with layers of ladyfingers soaked in espresso and mascarpone cream.
    Chocolate Lava Cake: Warm and decadent chocolate cake with a gooey molten center, served with vanilla ice cream.
    Crème Brûlée: Creamy vanilla custard with a caramelized sugar crust.
Beverages
    Soft Drinks: A selection of refreshing carbonated beverages.
    Freshly Brewed Iced Tea: Chilled black tea infused with a hint of lemon or peach.
    House-made Lemonade: Refreshing lemonade made with freshly squeezed lemons.

Please note that our menu items may vary based on seasonal availability and chef's specials.
