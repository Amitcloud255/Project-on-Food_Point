Delicious Summer Specials

Posted on August 25, 2023 by Food Shop Restaurant

Summer is here, and Food Shop Restaurant is thrilled to unveil our highly anticipated summer specials menu. Get ready to embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the vibrant flavors of the season.


Featured Dishes

Our summer specials menu boasts an array of innovative and mouthwatering dishes:

Grilled Mango Tango Salad - A tantalizing blend of juicy mangoes, mixed greens, grilled chicken, and a zesty citrus dressing.

Seaside Seafood Paella - Dive into a medley of shrimp, mussels, clams, and chorizo, all expertly simmered with saffron-infused rice.

Tropical Sunshine Smoothie Bowl - A refreshing breakfast delight with layers of tropical fruits, yogurt, granola, and a drizzle of honey.


Seasonal Ingredients

We take pride in sourcing the freshest locally grown produce for our dishes:

Tomatoes from a nearby farm to create the perfect base for our homemade marinara sauce.

Herbs like basil and mint grown in our own garden, adding aromatic accents to every plate.

Organic corn and bell peppers that add sweetness and crunch to our summer salads.


Refreshing Beverages

Beat the heat with our selection of rejuvenating beverages:

Watermelon-Mint Sparkler - A fizzy concoction of freshly pressed watermelon juice and muddled mint leaves.

Pineapple Coconut Breeze - A tropical blend of pineapple, coconut milk, and a hint of ginger, served over ice.

Cucumber-Lime Refresher - Cool off with this cucumber and lime infused water, a perfect hydrating companion to your meal.


Relaxing Ambiance

Our restaurant's serene ambiance is designed to enhance your dining experience:

Outdoor Seating: Enjoy your meal under the open sky, surrounded by lush greenery and the gentle rustling of leaves.

Live Music Evenings: Join us on Friday nights for live acoustic performances that set the mood for a perfect summer evening.

Visit Food Shop Restaurant today and savor the flavors of summer that will leave you craving for more. These specials are available for a limited time, so make the most of the season!
